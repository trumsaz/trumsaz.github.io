A Few Words About Us

Logistics company «Trums» is engaged in the organization and implementation of the various modes of transportation in all directions. We will deliver your goods to any place of the planet in the shortest possible time and with minimum cost. Transport and logistics company provides a full range of logistics services since 2007 year.Our work on many international destinations has allowed us to develop an extensive network of offices abroad.
